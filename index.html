Hallo! I'm Emily

I am a student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying Library and Information Science. When I grow up I want to work in an academic library teaching students how to research effectively!


I have my Bachelor of Arts in German and Teaching, and so I was interested in pursuing an internship in a German-speaking country. I was the intern at the Humboldt-Elsevier Advanced Data & Text Centre (HEADT Centre) in Berlin, Germany. I would like to thank the School of Information and the Center for European Studies at the University of Michigan for their financial support.
